About

The Beginning

Cup O' Dreams started with a simple dream — to create a space where people could come together, enjoy exceptional coffee, and escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. It all began in 2013 when Nico Diaz, fueled by passion and a love for coffee, opened the first Cup O' Dreams coffee shop in Phoenix, Arizona.

Our Vision

From the very beginning, our vision was clear — to be more than just a coffee shop. We wanted to create an environment where each cup served tells a story, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee inspires creativity, and where every visitor becomes a part of our extended family.

Growth and Innovation

Over the years, Cup O' Dreams has grown beyond our wildest dreams. With the support of our amazing community, we've expanded our offerings, introduced new and exciting blends, and embraced innovation while staying true to our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Thank You

As we reflect on our journey, we want to express our deepest gratitude to our loyal customers, dedicated team members, and everyone who has been a part of the Cup O' Dreams story. Your support has been the driving force behind our success, and we look forward to many more years of serving you the finest coffee and creating unforgettable moments together.
